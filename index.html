Who We Are

Malaysia's Pioneer in Robotics, AI & Automation

Established in 2017, the Malaysia Robotics, AI and Automation Association (MYRAS) is Malaysia's first and pioneering professional organisation dedicated to uniting key stakeholders across the robotics, automation, artificial intelligence, and advanced technology ecosystem.

Our Vision

A professional platform where all engineers and technologists can meet, network, and advance robotics and automation in Malaysia.

Our Mission

To advocate with the government on robotics and automation needs, and create awareness through conferences, seminars, and activities.

What We Offer

Our Programmes & Services

Connecting industry, academia, and government through impactful programmes that drive Malaysia's technology ecosystem.

💼
NRex
Industry & Career Platform

A dedicated platform connecting professionals with industry opportunities, career development resources, and talent engagement in robotics and AI.

🤖
RoTDA
Robotics & Technology Development

Advancing robotics and technology development in Malaysia through collaborative programmes, research partnerships, and applied innovation.

🔬
STEM RAI
STEM for Robotics, AI & Innovation

Inspiring the next generation through engaging STEM programmes focused on Robotics, AI, and innovation across schools and universities.

🧠
AI Programmes
AI for Robotics & Automation

Practical AI integration programmes for industry professionals, enabling organisations to harness the power of AI in their workflows.

🤝
Collaborations
Industry & Academic Partnerships

Bridging the gap between industry, academia, and government to create meaningful collaborations that accelerate technological advancement.

🌐
Global Network
International Engagement

Connecting Malaysian professionals with global robotics communities through IARA, GRC, and international conferences and exhibitions.
