Who We Are

Malaysia's Pioneer in Robotics, AI & Automation

Established in 2017, the Malaysia Robotics, AI and Automation Association (MYRAS) is Malaysia's first and pioneering professional organisation dedicated to uniting key stakeholders across the robotics, automation, artificial intelligence, and advanced technology ecosystem.

Our Vision

A professional platform where all engineers and technologists can meet, network, and advance robotics and automation in Malaysia.

Our Mission

To advocate with the government on robotics and automation needs, and create awareness through conferences, seminars, and activities.
